About us

Square Bracket Associates is dedicated to publishing free and open-source books about Squeak, Smalltalk and related topics.

Square Bracket Associates is not a publishing house. We are a group of individuals who are volunteering some of our time and effort to the release of open-source books that are in the interest of the Smalltalk community.
